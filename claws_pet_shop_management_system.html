Claws Pet Shop Management System

Project Overview

System Name: Claws Pet Shop Management System

Aims: To streamline pet shop operations by managing products, employees, customers, and billing in an organized system.

Technologies Used: C#, MS Access, Visual Studio

Development Time: December 2024 (Assignment of Windows Programming)

Features: -

  • Provided a dashboard displaying key metrics, including total products, employees, customers, and revenue.
  • Enabled staff to manage (view, add, modify, delete) products, employees, and customers.
  • Implemented a billing system that automatically updates inventory and records transaction history.

(1) Login Page
The login page allows pet shop staff to log into the system using their email and password, ensuring only authorized users can access the system.
(2) Home Page
The home page provides an overview of the system's key metrics. It displays four cards showing the total products, total employees, total customers, and total revenue, each provided with a button to link to each relevant section.
(2) Products Page
Under the "Products" tab, the Product Page displays a table with all products added to the system. It also includes a search function for users to filter products by category, making it easier to locate specific items.
(2) Add Product Page
This page allows staff to add new products by filling in details such as product name, selecting the product category, and entering the quantity and price.
(5) Modify Product Page
The Modify Product Page shows a list of all products in the system. The staff can search for a product by category, select a product from the table, and edit its details in the fields below or delete the selected product.
(6) Employees Page
The staff can add, modify, and delete employees directly from this page. It includes a table listing all employees and fields for adding new employee information. Employees added here will also be able to log into the system.
(7) Customers Page
The Customers page allows staff to view, add, modify, and delete customer records.
(8) Billing Page
This page allowed staff to process sales transactions. It features a billing form where staff can select a customer, pick products by selecting a row in the product table, and add them to the cart. Once the transaction is complete, a receipt is generated, and the inventory quantity is automatically updated to reflect the sale.
(9) Transaction History Page
This page provided a log of all past transactions. This page is read-only, allowing staff to view transaction details but not modify them.

Problem Solved by Claws Pet Shop Management System

1. Inventory Tracking

The system automatically updates product inventory after each sale, reducing the risk of errors caused by manual inventory management.

2. Sales and Transaction History Tracking

The transaction history feature helps keep track of all sales, ensuring transparency and easy access to past transactions for record-keeping and analysis.

Future Improvements

1. Mobile App Development

A mobile app could be developed to allow staff to manage the pet shop operations remotely, making the system more accessible and flexible for staff on the go.

2. Stock Management Enhancement

The system could be enhanced to automatically notify once stock levels fall below a certain threshold, ensuring that popular items are always in stock.
